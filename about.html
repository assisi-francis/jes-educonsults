Who We Are

JES Educational Consultants Ltd was established to provide expert consultancy services to help parents, students, and organizations across Nigeria and beyond with educational planning. We specialize in helping students achieve their dreams of studying at prestigious institutions around the world.

Our company is one of the leading educational consultants in the West African region with extensive expertise in navigating admissions into top-tier institutions in Australia, New Zealand, United States, United Kingdom, Canada, and the United Arab Emirates.

With over 10 years of experience and having successfully placed over 500 students in world-class universities, we pride ourselves on providing personalized, professional, and comprehensive support throughout your study abroad journey.

Our Mission

To empower students to achieve their academic and career aspirations by providing expert guidance, comprehensive support, and access to world-class educational opportunities abroad.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and preferred educational consultancy in West Africa, known for excellence, integrity, and transforming students' dreams into reality.
