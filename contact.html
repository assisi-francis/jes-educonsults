FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How long does the application process take?

The application process typically takes 4-8 weeks, depending on the institution and country. We work diligently to ensure timely submission and processing of all applications.

Do you charge for your services?

We offer free initial consultation. Our service fees vary depending on the destination and level of assistance required. Contact us for detailed pricing information.

What documents do I need?

Required documents typically include academic transcripts, passport, English proficiency test scores, personal statement, and reference letters. We provide a complete checklist during your consultation.

Can you help with scholarships?

Yes! We provide information on available scholarships and assist you in preparing strong scholarship applications to increase your chances of success.
